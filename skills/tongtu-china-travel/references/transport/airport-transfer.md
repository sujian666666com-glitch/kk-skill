# Airport Transfers — Getting from the Airport to the City

## TL;DR
- Every major Chinese airport has metro/rail connections — cheapest and most reliable
- Didi/taxi is more convenient with luggage but costs 3-10x more than metro
- All options accept Alipay QR or cash. Didi also accepts foreign credit cards directly
- Check which airport you're flying into — some major cities have 2 airports

## Quick Comparison

| Method | Cost range | Time | Payment | Best for |
|--------|-----------|------|---------|----------|
| **Metro/Airport Rail** | CNY 7-50 | 20-70 min | Alipay QR, contactless tap, cash | Budget travelers, solo, light luggage |
| **Airport Bus** | CNY 15-55 | 45-100 min | Cash, Alipay | Budget + direct hotel area drop-off |
| **Taxi/Didi** | CNY 80-350 | 30-80 min | Foreign cards (Didi), cash, Alipay | Groups, heavy luggage, late night |
| **Hotel shuttle** | Free-CNY 200 | Varies | Arranged by hotel | Premium hotels, zero hassle |

## Other Cities

This guide covers the most visited cities. For airports not listed above, the general pattern holds across China:

- **Metro/rail**: most mid-to-large city airports have a metro or rail link (CNY 5-15, 30-70 min)
- **Airport bus**: available at virtually all airports (CNY 15-30)
- **Taxi/Didi**: always available; use Didi for upfront pricing
- **How to find specifics**: search the airport name in Amap or Apple Maps for real-time transit options, or use `flyai ai-search` with a query like "how to get from [airport] to [city center]"

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What if I arrive late at night after the metro closes?**
Use Didi or take a taxi from the airport taxi queue. Airport taxi queues operate 24/7 at all major airports. Expect night surcharge (+20-30%).

**Should I arrange airport pickup through my hotel?**
If your hotel offers free shuttle service, take it. Paid hotel car service is more expensive than Didi but eliminates all hassle — worth it if you arrive exhausted or with heavy luggage.

**Can I use Didi at the airport?**
Yes. Search your airport name in Didi, select your terminal, and the app will automatically set the ride-hailing pickup zone as your starting point — with step-by-step walking directions and photos to guide you there. Wait times are usually 5-15 minutes.

**What about going TO the airport?**
Just set your departure terminal as the destination in Didi. Make sure you select the correct terminal — check your booking confirmation in advance, as major airports have multiple terminals.

**Do airports have currency exchange and ATMs?**
Yes, all international airports listed above have currency exchange counters (arrivals hall) and ATMs (Visa/Mastercard compatible). See `../payment/payment-overview.md` for details.

## Tips

- **Pre-plan your route** before landing — Amap and Apple Maps offer indoor maps for major airports, helping you navigate inside large terminals
- **Airport expressway tolls**: taxi/Didi fares from some airports include a highway toll (CNY 10-20) on top of the metered fare
- **Luggage on metro**: feasible but uncomfortable during rush hours. If you have 2+ large suitcases, take Didi
