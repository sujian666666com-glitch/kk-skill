## Description: <br>
Run a WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility audit on a design or page, including checks for contrast, keyboard navigation, touch targets, and screen reader behavior before handoff. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[rolandpg](https://clawhub.ai/user/rolandpg) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Designers, developers, and product teams use this skill to review designs or pages for WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility issues and produce prioritized remediation guidance before handoff. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Connected design or project-tracker tools could expose the wrong target or create unintended remediation tickets. <br>
Mitigation: Confirm the design or page being reviewed and require explicit approval before creating tickets or writing to external systems. <br>
Risk: Accessibility audit guidance may miss issues that require manual assistive-technology testing. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the audit as a starting point and verify important flows with keyboard navigation, color contrast checks, zoom testing, and screen readers such as VoiceOver or NVDA. <br>
